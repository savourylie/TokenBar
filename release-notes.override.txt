New:
- Reorder and hide client tabs. Drag providers into your preferred order in Settings and uncheck the ones you don't use; a hidden client vanishes from every number — tab bar, Overview totals, charts, menu-bar title and live rate, every report — and unhiding restores it instantly. Thanks @savourylie (#28).
- Hide the Agent limits card: a master switch, plus per-client toggles that keep the tab and its numbers. Useful for accounts with usage but no OAuth quota, like Claude Console. Thanks @yiskang (#33, #34).

Fixes:
- Dropped the phantom "<synthetic>" row from the Claude model breakdown — locally-fabricated zero-token turns; your totals are unchanged. Thanks @starburst3190 (#30).
- Hourly and Agents now filter per client in the core engine, so a single client's tab no longer shows whole-bucket totals for hours or agents it shares with other clients.
- Token sums saturate instead of overflowing on corrupt data.
